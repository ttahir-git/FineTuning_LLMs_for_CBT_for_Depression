3. **Risk Management**: Identify potential risks and develop contingency plans to mitigate them. This can include diversifying your revenue streams or finding alternative funding sources.

3. **Follow-Up**: After the meetings, send a summary of the discussion and any action items. This can help maintain accountability and keep everyone on track.

3. **Mindfulness Meditation**: Spend a few minutes each day practicing mindfulness meditation. This can help you stay grounded and present, reducing worries about the future.
